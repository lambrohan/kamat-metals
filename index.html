About Us

Kamat Metals is a pioneering company that design, manufacture and supply of superior quality Silver, Brass and Copper products like Drink ware, Kitchen ware, Pooja Sahitya, Corporate gifts, Home décor and many special articles.We are committed to improve manufacturing processes by various engineering solutions that make product attractive, beautiful, durable and exceptionally cost-effective. KAMAT METALS product innovations are focused cost and helping customers to increase their happiness. We always try to provide products which exceeds the satisfaction level of customer; a philosophy that is facilitated by the fact that the company sources only the finest materials and employs the latest technologies.

From the initial material inception stage, we do extensive design testing and quality checks. Testing of raw material plays an important role in quality of products. There are minimum 8 to maximum 17 processes involved in the journey of material from its raw stage to finished goods. The quality check is being carried out at all stage.

Dedicated to delivering product and quality excellence, Kamat Metal's business model and equipment portfolio have been created to respond to its customer's requirements to deliver ever-greater levels of quality and value. Our highly trained and dedicated team of engineers and skilled worker are using their analytical skills, practical experience and creative talents to provide leading edge to the product.

- History -

The journey started in the year of 1964. Mr. Madhav Gopal Kamat established Kamat Industries at Udyamnagar, Kolhapur. Initially Kamat Industries was the manufacturer of Iron products. With the continuous progress in market, Mr. Madhav Kamat entered in gunmetal and brass products. The next generation dreamed to enter in precious metals and dream came true in 1981. Mr. Anant Kamat and Mr. Gopal Kamat started manufacturing unit at Gujari, Kolhapur. They are the pioneer of various silver articles. Within 10 years, the portfolio of silver articles crossed hundred products. The price of silver crossed 80,000 per kilogram in the first decade of 21st century and common man was unable to afford silver articles. That time these two brothers started silver plated copper and brass articles. Now the purchasing habit is changing day by day and ecommerce plays an important role in industry. So the KAMAT METALS is established in year 2017 to serve customer directly. The third generation calculates amount drain by intermediates so that end customer suffers with high prices. The unethical practices are also carried by intermediates. So to avoid all those things and serve our valuable customers with quality and pure product, We entire Kamat Metals family working hard for the customer.
